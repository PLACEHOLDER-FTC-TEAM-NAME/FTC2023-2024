About Us

We are a First Tech Challenge team comprised of passionate and dedicated students who love all aspects of STEM. Our team is composed of talented individuals who share a common goal: to learn, innovate, and succeed in the field of robotics and other STEM related fields.

We believe that the future of technology lies in the hands of those who are willing to learn, experiment, and explore new ideas. That's why we've made it our mission to not only excel in robotics, but also to develop our skills in all STEM fields. We understand that in today's world, having a strong foundation in STEM is essential, and we are committed to doing our part to make a difference.

Skills

Our FTC team possesses a wide range of skills that we leverage to build and program our competitive robot.

Our programming team is well-versed in several programming languages, including Java, to develop code for our robot's autonomous and teleop functions.

The CAD team uses 3D modeling software to design and simulate robot parts and assemblies before we build them.

The build team uses power tools and various materials to bring our designs to life and assemble the robot.

Lastly, our notebook team meticulously documents our team's progress, reflections, and strategies in our engineering notebook, which we submit for judging at competitions.

Collectively, we collaborate to create a well-rounded robot that can perform various tasks and challenges no matter the competition.
